Prediction of Prostate Cancer Recurrence after Radical Prostatectomy

Welcome to RAPS (Risks After Prostate Surgery), a prediction tool that uses the personal characteristics of a patient after surgery for prostate cancer to estimate his probabilities of: a) prostate cancer recurrence, and b) dying without recurrence within 10 years of radical prostatectomy for his cancer.
